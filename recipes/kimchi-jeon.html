Kimchi Jeon

Description

Kimchi jeon is a beloved Korean dish that exemplifies the art of transforming kimchi into a delightful pancake. Made by mixing chopped or thinly sliced kimchi with a simple batter of flour, water, and sometimes egg, kimchi jeon boasts a unique combination of flavors and textures.

The fermentation of kimchi infuses the pancake with a tangy and slightly spicy essence, while the batter crisps up beautifully when cooked in a hot pan with a drizzle of oil. This results in a pancake that is crispy on the outside yet tender and flavorful on the inside, offering a satisfying contrast with each bite.

Ingredients

  • 3 green onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 tbsp chili powder
  • Pinch of sugar
  • Splash of vinegar
  • Kimchi + Kimchi juice

Steps

  1. Cut all the green onions, garlic, and kimchi
  2. Mix until the batter is fairly thick, not too runny
  3. Cook with some oil
  4. Flip until both sides brown on the edges.
  5. Enjoy!
