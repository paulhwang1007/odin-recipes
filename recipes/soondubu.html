Soondubu

Description

Soondubu jjigae, also known as soft tofu stew, is a popular Korean dish known for its spicy and comforting flavors. It is cherished not only for its bold flavors and heartwarming qualities, but also for its versatility and ability to comfort both body and soul.

Because of its versatility, soondubu jjigae can consist of any combination of ingredients that is desired, but generally consists of a flavorful broth, soft tofy, spicy seasoning, protein, vegetables, egg, and more. The recipe specified on this page is just one example of how one may enjoy this amazing dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of silken tofu
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 bunch of mushroom of choice: enoki, shimeji
  • 1/2 zucchini
  • Protein of choice: beef, seafood
  • 1.5 tbsp chili powder
  • 1.5 tbsp chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 egg
  • 200 ml of water or anchovy stock
  • Salt and MSG to taste

Steps

  1. Dice onion, green onion, garlic
  2. Simmer in pot with sesame oil
  3. Put in chili powder and chili flakes
  4. Pour in water/stock and bring to low simmer
  5. Once simmering, put in mushroom, zucchini, protein, and season to taste
  6. Boil and before serving, put in tofu and egg.
  7. Enjoy!
